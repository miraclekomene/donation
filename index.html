Welcome to our charity

Bring Water to the kids

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right, yet millions of children around the world still lack this basic necessity. At Nawiri, we are committed to changing this reality by providing sustainable water solutions to communities in need. Through our initiatives, we aim to build wells, install water filtration systems, and educate communities on the importance of hygiene and sanitation. By bringing clean water to children, we not only improve their health and well-being but also empower them to pursue education and a brighter future. Join us in our mission to make clean water accessible to every child.

Support Education for All

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and unlocking a brighter future for children around the world. At Nawiri, we believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our programs focus on building schools, providing learning materials, and training teachers in underserved communities. By supporting education for all, we empower children with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in life and contribute positively to their communities. Join us in our mission to ensure that every child has the opportunity to learn and grow.
