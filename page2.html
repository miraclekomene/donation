About Us

Our Mission to Help the Poor

Nawiri was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to alleviate poverty and create sustainable change in underserved communities around the world.

We believe that every person deserves access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive. Through our various programs and initiatives, we work directly with communities to identify their most pressing needs and develop tailored solutions.

Our approach focuses on empowerment rather than dependency, ensuring that the communities we serve can continue to grow and prosper long after our direct involvement ends.

Eradicating Poverty

Welcome to Our Charity

Since our founding, we have impacted the lives of over 50,000 people across 15 countries. Our work focuses on three key areas: education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.

We partner with local organizations and community leaders to ensure our programs are culturally appropriate and address the specific needs of each community.

Your support enables us to continue this vital work and expand our reach to more communities in need.
